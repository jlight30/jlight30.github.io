About Me

My name is Jennifer Light and I am a third year undergraduate student at Georgia Tech majoring in Computational Media.

I just recently joined the Computational Media Major and having a lot of fun learning about about computer science, websites, and design. I love creating art, and am hoping to integrate my creativity into my future work. My favorite medium is oil paint, and my favorite subject to paint are portraits. I have recently entered the world of digital art, and am slowly becoming more comfortable with mediums like Photoshop and Animate.

For my first two years here at Tech, I was studying Earth and Atmospheric Sciences. While I eventually realized that path wasn't right for me, it has taught me a lot about our Earth and grown my passion for sustainability.

Outside of school, one of my favorite things to do in my free time is play volleyball. I joined the club volleyball team my second year at Georgia Tech and it has been one of my best decisions. It gives me the oppotunity to meet new friends and get exercise while playing a sport that I love.
